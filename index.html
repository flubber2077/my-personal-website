About the Website

This is a website built to display my basic proficiencies with HTML, Javascript, CSS, and overall website creation abilities. I am also hoping to use this project as a exercise in learning website design, i.e. how to make it look like it was made sometime after 2002. The main struggle will be figuring out a way to make it interactive in such a way that Javascript will be a useful tool.

About the Author

Before the current pivot into programming, I studied Music Technology in the Appalachian mountains at the University of North Carolina-Asheville. I went on to build and repair specialty musical instruments at Moog Music. In August of 2021, I finished my time at Moog and bought a plane ticket to Spain.

Hiking El Camino Frances

The Camino de Santiago, or Way of St. James is a thousand+ year old pilgrimage that ends in Santiago de Compostela, the capital of the autonomous region of Galicia in northwest Spain. I hiked along the most popular route, the Camino Frances, which I started at the border of France.
